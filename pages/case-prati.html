Case Study · 01 — Strategic Branding

Prati
Assicurazioni

Client

Prati & C.
Consulenze Assicurative
Bagnolo Mella, Brescia

Scope

Brand Identity · Logo · Web Design · Campaign Strategy · Copywriting · Interior Design

Year

2024–2025

01 — Challenge

Starting from Zero

Prati & C. Consulenze Assicurative is a young insurance consultancy based in Bagnolo Mella, Brescia. When we started working together, they had no brand at all — no logo, no visual language, no digital presence.

The brief was as open as it gets: be original, stand out from the big players — Generali, Allianz — who they collaborate with but whose visual identity reads as dated and corporate. A young team, a human approach, a local reality that wanted to be remembered.

They gave me full creative freedom. That kind of trust is rare, and it deserves to be honored with real work.

02 — Brand Identity

The Mark

The brand name is the founders' surname — Prati. The logo builds on the letter P, into which I embedded a graphic symbol recalling an angel's wing — a subtle, elegant reference to the core concept of the business: protection.

The color palette is built around blue and white — blue for security and trust, white for clarity and approachability. The typography pairs a refined serif for the brand name with a modern sans-serif payoff, balancing institutional weight with contemporary lightness.

For the physical space, I introduced a third color: yellow. Used selectively on furniture, lamps, and accent pieces, it brings energy and warmth — because this is a young agency run by real people, not a faceless corporation.

03 — Campaign

The Animal Kingdom

For the advertising campaign, I developed a cast of animal characters — each one embodying a different insurance product through metaphor. The approach is warm, witty, and completely unexpected in the insurance sector.

A turtle sheltering under an umbrella in a hailstorm for home insurance. A raccoon dressed as a thief for car coverage. A squirrel passing an acorn to a smaller one for savings plans. An elderly wolf and his companion for long-term care. A horse with a bandaged leg for health insurance.

Each character was built through AI generation and my own post-production — every detail refined, every expression designed. I also wrote all the headlines and body copy for the entire campaign, from concept through to the final word on every poster.

"An entire agency in a single person — from the brand mark to the walls of the office."
04 — Result

What Was Built

A complete brand system — logo, color palette, typography, brand guidelines — extended across every touchpoint: website, social media templates, print materials, business cards, signage, and the physical design of the office space.

The large-format campaign posters hang on the studio walls, turning the workspace itself into a brand statement. The result is a coherent environment where every element — from the screen to the chairs — tells the same story.

A single creative direction, a single point of contact, a complete and consistent identity. From the first digital impression to the physical experience of walking through the door.
