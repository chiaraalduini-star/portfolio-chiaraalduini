01 — Brief

The Brand Behind the Product

Two connected briefs, one visual world. Latteria Alta Italia is a local Italian dairy brand that needed a logo with the warmth and authority of a classic — modern enough to work today, timeless enough to outlast trends.

Local Farm is an artisanal yogurt and milk producer requiring a complete logotype and packaging system that could communicate freshness, locality, and craft quality at a glance.

02 — The Approach

Modern Vintage

For Latteria Alta Italia, the design direction was modern vintage — a logo that references the visual language of Italian dairy tradition without becoming nostalgic pastiche. Clean geometry, considered letterforms, and a restrained palette that works on labels, stamps, and packaging alike.

For Local Farm, the packaging system was built around hand-drawn botanical illustrations — fruits, herbs, and natural elements that change per flavour while maintaining a consistent brand framework. Each yogurt variety has its own illustration, its own colour, its own personality — unified by the same typographic and structural logic.
