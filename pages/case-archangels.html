The Archangels
A Personal Series

Client

Post-Anthropocene
Personal Practice

Scope

Digital Collage · Photography · Art Direction

Year

2023 – ongoing

01 — Concept

Sacred Figures, Contemporary Eyes

The Archangels reinterprets Gabriel, Michael, Raphael, and Uriel through a contemporary lens. Shared across Christianity, Islam, and Judaism, these figures are presented as symbolic, modern presences. Each name appears in Italian, Hebrew, and Arabic — a reminder of their universal nature.

02 — Process

Constructed Image

Each piece is built in Photoshop through photography, digital painting, and layered composition. Fashion aesthetics meet baroque structure — drapery, light, and simplified symbols in dialogue.

03 — Post-Anthropocene

Personal Practice

Part of Chiara Alduini's ongoing personal practice, exploring spirituality, identity, and image through digital collage and photography.

04 — Reference

Historical Sources

Each work combines a photographic figure with a historical iconographic source. For Michael, Guido Reni's Saint Michael Defeats Satan (1636) informs pose and composition, translated into a contemporary visual language.
