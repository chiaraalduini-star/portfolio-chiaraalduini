Scope

Illustration · Infographic Design · Brochure · Video · Web

Year

N/D

01 — Brief

Making Technology Approachable

Kayma is a tool designed to calculate and adjust temperature in apartment buildings with central heating — a technically complex product that needed to be communicated clearly to a non-technical audience.

The brief from Borgo Creativo, a Brescia-based creative agency, was to develop a complete illustration system for use across a technical brochure, promotional video, and website. Every visual had to make the invisible logic of the product immediately readable.

02 — The Work

A Complete Illustration System

Each illustration was designed to explain a specific function or benefit of the Kayma system — from installation to data reading, from individual apartment control to building-wide management. The visual language is clean, flat, and consistent across all touchpoints.
