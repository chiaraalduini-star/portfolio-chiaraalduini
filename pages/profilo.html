About

Agency‑in‑a‑Box

I'm Chiara Alduini, an independent Creative Director with 20+ years of international experience.

I work with SMEs, cultural institutions, NGOs and embassies to turn complex ideas into complete, recognizable visual identities — logo, website, campaigns, infographics, illustrations, physical spaces.

I don't just deliver a logo. I build a brand's complete visual language and bring it everywhere it needs to be.
